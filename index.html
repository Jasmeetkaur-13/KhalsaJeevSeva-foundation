About Khalsa Jeev Seva

Khalsa Jeev Seva is a charitable organization dedicated to the welfare of animals, embodying Sikh values of selfless service and righteousness. It provides shelter and medical care to animals in need and compassion for all living beings. The organization aims to create a compassionate world where animals are treated with dignity, love, and respect.

Our Mission

H**Our Mission** Our mission is to elevate animal welfare through compassion and dedicated service. We empower individuals with the knowledge and advocacy needed to inspire collective action, fostering a culture of respect. Together, we strive for a future where every animal is honored and protected.

"The animals are not ours to exploit. They are our friends, and we should treat them with compassion."
– Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
